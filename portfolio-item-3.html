Simple Covid19 Website

Tracking Canada dataset

Alognquin College
March 2021 - March 2021 (within 1 month)

Having completed one simple Covid19 Canada data tracking using Python and Django framework, with Bootstrap for templates and PostgreSQL for DBMS.

    Provide user the ability to keep track of covid cases in Canada at a specific province or time.
    Provide the ability to upload the dataset as well as to filter the records due to multiple fields such as: name, date range.
    Support C-R-U-D operations and will develop to display on the Data Chart in the future.
